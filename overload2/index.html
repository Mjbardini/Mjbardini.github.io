Power Overload

Instructions


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your current score to the target number. You will lose the game if your current score goes above the targeted number.

The assigned values of each crystal will change after each round.
